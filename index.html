The Next Generation Anonymous Cryptonote Based Cryptocurrency

Decentralized cryptocurrencies are slowly becoming a part of normal everyday life, adapting to create a new revolutionary economy based on digital money. Alloy allows anyone to send money digitally over our decentralized blockchain instantly, anywhere in the world, to anyone in the world.

A private, fast and efficient Cryptocurrency based on CryptoNote.

Secure

Each transaction is secured with robust cryptography and distributed through a global peer-to-peer consensus network.

Private

Alloy utilizes a cryptographic system to transfer funds without exposing any information of any user on the blockchain.

Untraceable

Alloy uses ring signatures to make transactions untraceable, meaning it is very difficult for blockchain analysis to determine if funds have been spent.
